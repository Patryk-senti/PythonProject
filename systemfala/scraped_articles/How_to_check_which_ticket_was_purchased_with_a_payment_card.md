# How to check which ticket was purchased with a payment card?


Go to the systemfala.pl portal, then in the upper right corner, next to the log in button, select the payment card option  
The system will redirect you to the form. Enter the appropriate data in the form and check which ticket was purchased.




Link: https://systemfala.pl/help/system-fala/jak-sprawdzic-jaki-bilet-zosta-zakupiony-na-karte-patnicza
